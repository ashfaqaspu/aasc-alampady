AASC Alampady

Alampady Arts & Sports Club (AASC) is a community-driven institution rooted in unity, discipline, and social responsibility. With a legacy spanning nearly four decades, AASC stands as a proud pillar of cultural, athletic, and humanitarian growth in Alampady.

About AASC

AASC is dedicated to nurturing talent, leadership, and collective progress through arts, sports, and service. Built on systematic growth and inclusive participation, the club provides a platform where creativity flourishes, discipline is cultivated, and values are strengthened.

Commitment to Society

Charity at AASC is a continuous responsibility, not an occasional activity. The club believes that true development emerges when sports, culture, and service move together in harmony.

Our History & Legacy

Registered on 27 November 1991 and registered under Reg No. 255/91, NYK.012, Govt. of India, AASC has evolved into a respected institution known for discipline, leadership, and service.

Community & Charity

With the formation of the GCC Committee in 2016, AASC expanded its humanitarian reach internationally, strengthening year-round charity and social welfare initiatives.

Our Strength

  • Nearly 400 active members worldwide
  • Strong GCC presence across countries
  • Structured Central & GCC Committees
  • Government-recognized registered organization
  • Year-round sports, cultural & charity programs

AASC Own Building

A Permanent Home for Vision, Growth & Service

On 11 January 2026, AASC achieved a historic milestone with the inauguration of its own building on its own land — a symbol of unity, dedication, and long-term vision.

Inaugurated by Professor Gopinath Muthukad, marking the realization of a long-cherished dream.

More than a structure, the building is a living space for training, creativity, charity, leadership, and community engagement — ensuring stability and independence for future generations.

Our Vision

To build a society where sports inspire discipline, charity strengthens humanity, and unity shapes a better future — Better Together.
