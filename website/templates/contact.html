About AASC Alampady

Alampady Arts & Sports Club (AASC) is a community-driven organization based in Alampady, Kasaragod district, Kerala. For decades, AASC has been actively involved in sports development, social responsibility, charity initiatives, and community welfare activities.

The club stands as a trusted support system for the local community, responding to social needs with unity, discipline, and compassion.

Blood Donation Support

AASC Alampady operates an active blood support network for the Alampady and nearby regions. In case of medical emergencies, accidents, or urgent blood requirements, our volunteers coordinate quickly to arrange donors and assistance.

This service is available round-the-clock and is purely driven by humanitarian values to save lives within our locality.

Medical Aid Support

AASC Alampady also extends support for urgent medical assistance, including guidance, coordination, medical equipment relief and financial help for patients from Alampady and surrounding areas who are facing critical health situations.

Our medical aid initiatives focus on timely support, community cooperation, and ensuring that no one is left alone during medical emergencies.
